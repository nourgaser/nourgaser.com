Pixel art step-based strategy roguelike game on a hexagonal grid.

Root People

A PvP 2D platformer.

A Brawlhalla-inspired game where you play as a root person and fight other root people. The map contains patches of grass which different heros can use to their advantage, each in their own way.

This was sadly an incomplete submission for the 2022 Global Game Jam due to time constraints. I plan to finish it in the future.
